# /virtue-review

The Stoics named four cardinal virtues. They are not abstract. They are *practices* that you have either rehearsed this week or let atrophy.

Run this in 5 to 10 minutes.

## The audit

For each virtue, the user gives:
1. A score from 1 (didn't show up at all) to 5 (paid the price willingly).
2. A single specific example, positive or negative.

### Courage

The virtue of moving toward fear, danger, and truth.

- Where did I say the hard thing this week?
- Where did I ask the uncomfortable question?
- Where did I back down or sugarcoat?
- Score (1-5): ___
- Example: ___

### Discipline

The virtue of self-mastery. Doing the harder right.

- Where did I pick hard over easy this week?
- Where did I keep a promise to myself?
- Where did I break one?
- Score (1-5): ___
- Example: ___

### Justice

The virtue of treating others well. Fairness, service, fidelity.

- Who did I serve well this week?
- Who did I fail? What is the repair?
- Did I speak about absent people the way I would if they were in the room?
- Score (1-5): ___
- Example: ___

### Wisdom

The virtue of seeing clearly. Rethinking, updating, humility.

- What did I see more clearly this week than I did last week?
- Where was I wrong and admitted it?
- What am I still fooling myself about?
- Score (1-5): ___
- Example: ___

## The weakest virtue

Identify the lowest-scored virtue.

Ask: "For the coming week, what is one specific action that would rehearse this virtue?"

Examples:
- Courage → "I will have the conversation with <person> about <topic> by Thursday."
- Discipline → "I will not drink alcohol this week." Or "I will get up at 6am every day."
- Justice → "I will write the thank-you note I have been putting off. I will call my mom Sunday."
- Wisdom → "I will name one opinion I hold strongly and argue the opposite side for 20 minutes in writing."

## Output

Append to `~/adulting-data/journal/virtue-log.md`:

```
# <date>

Courage:    <score>/5  — <example>
Discipline: <score>/5  — <example>
Justice:    <score>/5  — <example>
Wisdom:     <score>/5  — <example>

Focus virtue next week: <one>
Rehearsal: <specific action, due date>
```

Then print a one-line summary. Not a lecture. The scoring is the teacher.
